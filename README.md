## The Problem

People are using AI to write - but everything comes out sounding the same. Formal. Sanitized. Hollow. Your audience can tell. And when you try to "add your voice back," you're rewriting AI output line by line anyway.

## What Timbre Does

Timbre captures the way you naturally write — your tone, rhythm, word choice, style - and uses it as the foundation for every piece of content it generates.

You write once. Timbre learns. Everything after that sounds like you.

**Works for:**
- LinkedIn posts that get engagement instead of silence
- Cold emails that get replies instead of ignores
- Resumes that feel human, not templated
- Portfolios that reflect your actual personality

---

## How It Works

1. **Feed it your writing** — past posts, essays, anything you've written
2. **Timbre builds your voice profile** — tone, structure, vocabulary, cadence
3. **Generate content** — it drafts in your voice, not a generic one
